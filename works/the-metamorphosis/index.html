The Origin of Kafka’s Metamorphosis

THE METAMORPHOSIS

Kafka’s book The Metamorphosis is illustrated through typography and symbolic representation. Much like Gregor, the letters are trapped within the body of an overwhelmingly large form and with it large a burden. His mind and body being overshadowed by his hopelessness.
