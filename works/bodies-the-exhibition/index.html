A poster series designed with a different approach in highlighting an exhibition that showcases human bodies preserved through a process called plastination. The exhibit displays different systems, active poses, and various stages of development. In this poster series I have depicted these shocking forms into imagery that represents the body and presents itself in curious ways.

24in x 36in | December 2020
