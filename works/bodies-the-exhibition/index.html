An uncensored view of the human body

BODIES: THE EXHIBITION

A poster series designed with a different approach in highlighting an exhibition that showcases humanbodies preserved through a process called plastination. The exhibit displays different systems, activeposes, and various stages of development. In this poster series I have depicted these shocking forms into imagery that represents the body andpresents itself in curious ways creating 3D models for imagery. This compelling imagery brings attention to my subject and provides a new view on a sensitive topic.

OBJECTIVES

  • Explore the drawing process as a powerful tool for the generation, exploration, + critical refinement of ideas
  • Examine a variety of media options + drawing techniques
