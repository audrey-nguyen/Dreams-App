Branding & Identity Design

PICTORIAL PORTRAIT

In this project I investigated ways to re-design and represent a local plant shop located in Chicago. I chose to give the logo a more modern yet fun quirky approach by using sans-serif and playing with the interaction between type and image. In the poster I focused on using a mixture of painted images and photographs in a collaged 'scenery' to encapsulate the dense foliage and peculiar atmosphere.

OBJECTIVES

  • Explore imaginative ways to represent the subject visually
  • Investigate pictorial symbolism to communicate ideas
  • Create a coherent, compelling visual narrative
16.5in x 23in | October 2020
