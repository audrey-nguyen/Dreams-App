CRIZARD

Collaborative Game Challenge

This is a 48 hour game making marathon, focused on design, mechanics, and clever ideas. Games are judged by the public. My team and I focused on a run and gun type video game. Our main idea centered around a crow who gained mystical powers and attempts to destory the evil around him but eventually finds that he loses control. While the others worked on coding, mechanics and etc, Eve focused on character design and pixel art, and I illustrated the setting along with some design work.

  • Theme: Out of Control
  • Time Frame: 48hrs
  • Team Breadcrumbs: 3 Coders | 2 Artists/Designers
GMTK Game Jam 2020
