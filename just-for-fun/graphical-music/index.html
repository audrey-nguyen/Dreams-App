Graphical Music Sketches

While listening to these classical pieces of music I arranged the piece into a quick visual data. I focused on illustrating the main lines and timbre, but it also includes tempo, dynamics and varied repetition. Overall I've arranged these pieces in what seemed to fit in the moment of the music.
